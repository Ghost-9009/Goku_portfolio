About Me

I turn fleeting moments into lasting art.

With over a decade behind the lens, I've photographed weddings across 6 continents, portraits of world-renowned artists, and campaigns for global brands. My approach is candid, emotive, and deeply human.

I believe every photograph tells a story — and my job is to make sure it's yours. Whether it's the joy of a first look, the raw energy of a live performance, or the quiet beauty of a landscape, I'm there to capture it.
